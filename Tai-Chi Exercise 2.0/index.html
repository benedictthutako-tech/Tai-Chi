Professional Biography

Ros is a British Chartered Physiotherapist who trained in 1977 in Ontario, Canada. Following graduating with a Diploma in 1977 and early clinical experience there across inpatient and outpatient settings, hydrotherapy, and community practice she returned to the UK. Ros continued her career within the NHS where she worked in older people’s care and day hospitals prior to specialising in musculoskeletal outpatients and women’s health...
