My German YouTube experience

December 7, 2020

It was my pleasure to join the BVTQ Das Netzwerk YouTube channel recently to take part in a presentation on “Supporting Parkinson’s patients with Taijiquan and Qigong”.

The video features interviews with three Tai Chi experts from across the globe about their experiences using the art form with Parkinson’s patients.

It was very interesting to learn how other people were using Tai Chi to help their patients, and to make my media debut on the international stage.
